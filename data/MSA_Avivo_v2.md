# MASTER SERVICE AGREEMENT
**Document ID:** MSA-2026-882
**Effective Date:** January 15, 2026

## Section 1: Services Provided
AVIVO Consulting ("Provider") agrees to provide Lead Data Science consulting services, including the development of AI-driven contract intelligence pipelines, to GlobalLogistics Inc. ("Client").

## Section 2: Payment Terms
Client shall pay Provider at the rate of $250 per hour for services rendered. Invoices will be submitted monthly and are due within net 45 days of receipt. Late payments will incur a penalty of 5% per month.

## Section 3: Limitation of Liability
IN NO EVENT SHALL EITHER PARTY BE LIABLE FOR ANY INDIRECT, INCIDENTAL, SPECIAL, PUNITIVE, OR CONSEQUENTIAL DAMAGES. Provider's total liability under this Agreement shall not exceed the total amount paid by the Client in the three (3) months preceding the incident.

## Section 4: Termination
Either party may terminate this Agreement without cause upon sixty (60) days written notice to the other party. In the event of a material breach, the non-breaching party may terminate this agreement with immediate effect if the breach is not cured within 15 days of written notice.

## Section 5: Intellectual Property
All code, models, and intellectual property developed by the Provider during the course of this engagement shall become the exclusive property of the Client upon full payment of all outstanding invoices.
